Description

The sidebar allows you to highlight certain sections of your course, as well as general information, like the course code, the instructor, and semester the course is offered.

The sidebar contains three main components. The header, the nav, and a search of the nav. The sidebar to your left is an example of that. To just get the area for the sidebar and the content, all you need is the following HTML

<div class="sidebar">
  <!-- place your sidebar content here -->
</div>
<div class="main">
  <!-- place your main content here -->
</div>

Customizations

The following are the defaults for the custom attributes you can set on the sidebar We provide a set of recommended customizations in customizations. You can change these settings in the :root attribute in the course-pages.css file. 

--sidebar-bg-color: #F7F5E6;
--sidebar-text-color: #52658F;
--sidebar-link-color: #333A56;
