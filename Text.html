  1. Headers

Headers

We recommend using a maximum of 4 depths for the header (h1 through h4) and no more. Any more than this may mean that the content needs to be restructured. Too many sub-headlines may confuse your readers.

<h1 class="f1">Largest Header</h1>
<h2 class="f2">Smaller</h2>
<h3 class="f3">Even Smaller</h3>
<h4 class="f3">Recommended Smallest</h4>

Paragraph

Paragraph text should have line height and measure. Line height is the spacing between lines, while measure is how wide a line is. We recommend 1.5 line height and a wide measure to ensure easy readability.

<p class="measure-wide lh-copy">Text goes here</p>

For large screen sizes, we recommend that it have a margin left of the screen or sidebar, like so:

<p class="ml5 measure-wide lh-copy">Text goes here</p>

Links

Links should always be colored blue. Blue for links is historic, and provides good contrast with white. The following is a snippet for links

<a href="url goes here" class="link blue">Text</a>
